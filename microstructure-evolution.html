Microstructure Evolution

Visual study tool for how Pb-Sn microstructures change during cooling

Credit: Microstructure images and supporting material derived from Megan Dawkins (Spring 2018).

How to Use This Page

Follow the progression from liquid → two-phase regions → final solid structure. Use these visuals alongside the phase diagram to understand how microstructures form during cooling.

Phase Regions and Microstructures
